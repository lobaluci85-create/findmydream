FindMyDream ("we", "us") is operated by [YOUR NAME / COMPANY], contactable at [CONTACT EMAIL].
This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have.

## What we collect
- **Your Pi identity.** When you sign in with Pi, we receive your Pi user ID and username
  from Pi Network. We do not see your Pi password or wallet keys.
- **Your dreams.** The dream text you write, the date, and the symbols/archetype the app
  derives from it, so we can store your journal and show your history.
- **Payment records.** When you buy a deep reading, we store the Pi payment identifier and
  transaction ID to confirm the purchase. The payment itself is processed by Pi Network on
  the Pi blockchain.
- We do **not** collect your name, email, contacts, location, or device identifiers, and we
  use no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs beyond what Pi provides.

## How we use it
- To run the service: save your journal, show your history and the "on this day" look-back,
  and unlock purchased readings.
- To produce the **collective** view: we count how often each symbol appears across all users
  and show only **aggregated, anonymized totals** (e.g. "4,120 people dreamed of water").
  Your individual dreams are never shown to anyone else.
- If AI deep readings are enabled, your dream text is sent to our AI provider [PROVIDER] solely
  to generate that reading. [State retention terms of your provider.]

## Sharing
We do not sell your data. We share only: aggregated, non-identifying symbol counts (the
collective view); information necessary to process your Pi payment (with Pi Network); and the
AI provider above, if enabled. We may disclose data if required by law.

## Storage & retention
Your dreams are stored until you delete them or your account. We keep data only as long as
needed to provide the service.

## Your rights
You can, at any time, from within the app:
- **Access / export** your data (a full copy of your dreams).
- **Delete** any single dream, all your dreams, or your entire account and payment history.
Under UK/EU GDPR you also have the right to object to or restrict processing and to lodge a
complaint with your data protection authority (in the UK, the ICO).

secure; please don't write anything in a dream entry you wouldn't want stored.
