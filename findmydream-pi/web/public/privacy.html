Privacy Policy — FindMyDream

Last updated: 28 June 2026

FindMyDream ("we", "us") is operated by TheNakedBusiness, contactable at TheNakedBussines@gmail.com. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have.

What we collect

  • Your Pi identity. When you sign in with Pi, we receive your Pi user ID and username from Pi Network. We do not see your Pi password or wallet keys.
  • Your dreams. The dream text you write, the date, and the symbols/archetype the app derives from it, so we can store your journal and show your history.
  • Payment records. When you buy a deep reading, we store the Pi payment identifier and transaction ID to confirm the purchase. The payment itself is processed by Pi Network on the Pi blockchain.
  • We do not collect your name, email, contacts, location, or device identifiers, and we use no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs beyond what Pi provides.

How we use it

  • To run the service: save your journal, show your history and the "on this day" look-back, and unlock purchased readings.
  • To produce the collective view: we count how often each symbol appears across all users and show only aggregated, anonymized totals (e.g. "4,120 people dreamed of water"). Your individual dreams are never shown to anyone else.
  • If AI deep readings are enabled, your dream text is sent to our AI provider Anthropic (Claude) solely to generate that reading. The provider does not train on this data and does not retain it beyond generating the reading.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share only: aggregated, non-identifying symbol counts (the collective view); information necessary to process your Pi payment (with Pi Network); and the AI provider above, if enabled. We may disclose data if required by law.

Storage & retention

Your dreams are stored until you delete them or your account. We keep data only as long as needed to provide the service.

Your rights

You can, at any time, from within the app: - Access / export your data (a full copy of your dreams). - Delete any single dream, all your dreams, or your entire account and payment history. Under UK/EU GDPR you also have the right to object to or restrict processing and to lodge a complaint with your data protection authority (in the UK, the ICO).

Children

FindMyDream is not intended for children under 18. Do not use it if you are under that age.

Security

We protect data with reasonable technical and organizational measures. No system is perfectly secure; please don't write anything in a dream entry you wouldn't want stored.

Changes

We'll update this page and the date above when this policy changes.

Contact

Questions or requests: TheNakedBussines@gmail.com.
